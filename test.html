ACCT 210 Accounting for Managers (3 credits)

Offered by: Management (Desautels Faculty of Ma nagement)

Overview

Accounting : An introduction to financial and managerial accounting from the user perspective. Accounting concepts, elements of finan cial statements, including analysis and interpretation thereof. Behaviour of costs, cost-volume-profit analysis, job and standard costs, budgeting and performance m easurement, relevant costs for decision making, evaluation of long term investments.
